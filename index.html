OUR APPROACH

Luxury should feel lived in, not untouchable.

Every VELUNERA collection is developed with longevity in mind. We value thoughtful construction, timeless silhouettes and materials chosen to age beautifully with wear.

Our goal isn't simply to create beautiful products. It's to create pieces people continue reaching for, season after season, because they feel authentic, dependable and effortlessly elegant.

THE HOUSE OF VELUNERA

Building a lasting identity through fashion, beauty and fragrance.

Founded by Mrs Michelle Bermuda, VELUNERA was created with the belief that luxury should feel personal rather than distant. Every collection reflects a commitment to thoughtful craftsmanship, timeless design and everyday elegance.

From tailored clothing to skincare, botanical oils and fragrance, each release is designed to complement the others, creating a complete lifestyle rather than a collection of separate products.

The house continues to grow while remaining true to its original purpose: creating pieces that people choose because they feel beautiful, practical and lasting.
